Features

Everything you need for
wound assessment.

From accurate measurement to treatment selection — WoundPilot streamlines your entire wound care workflow so you can focus on your patients.

3D Wound Measurement

Point your iPhone at the wound and measure length, width, and area directly on the skin surface — even on curved areas like knees and elbows.

Etiology Determination

Answer a short clinical questionnaire and WoundPilot suggests the wound type — with clear reasoning you can review and verify before proceeding.

Healing Progress Tracking

PUSH scoring tracks whether wounds are improving over time, with visual trajectory charts that make progress reviews quick and clear.

Dressing Recommendations

Get evidence-based dressing suggestions tailored to wound type, exudate level, and tissue condition — with allergy checks built in.

Patient Management

Maintain patient profiles with medical history, comorbidities, and a complete wound history you can review across visits.

Secure & Private

Face ID login, automatic session timeout, and encrypted storage keep patient data protected to healthcare standards.

Security & Compliance

Patient data, protected.

01

Face ID Login

Unlock the app with Face ID. Automatic session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity keeps data safe if you step away.

02

Your Data, Only Yours

Each practitioner's patient data is completely isolated. No one else can access your patients — not even other WoundPilot users.

03

GDPR Aware

Full account deletion, no unnecessary data collection, and on-device processing mean your patients' privacy is respected.
